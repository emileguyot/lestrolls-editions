Gameplay

Features

🌠

20 Hand-Crafted Waves

Every wave has its own music, skybox, and unique gameplay mechanic. From stealth fields to gravity wells, no two waves play the same.

💥

Epic Boss Battles

Face the Guardian's orbiting asteroid shield at Wave 10, then take on the 4-phase Planet Breaker in the ultimate showdown at Wave 20.

⚡

8 Stackable Powerups

Shield, Rapid Fire, Spread Shot, Instant Kill, and more. Combine them for devastating combos like the 6-bullet barrage.

🎧

20 Original Music Tracks

Each wave features its own composed soundtrack, with smooth crossfade transitions as you progress deeper into the asteroid belt.

📡

Radio Narrative

Typewriter-style transmissions from HQ and your Ship AI tell the story of the missing Ariadne crew as you navigate the Kepler-442 system.

🎮

Retro Easter Egg

Discover the hidden classic 2D Asteroids game with chiptune music. A love letter to the 1979 original, right inside the game.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Les Trolls Editions ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how our games handle your data.

Data We Collect

Our games collect no personal data. We do not track you, sell your information, or use analytics.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Game Center: If you sign in to Game Center, Apple handles your player profile and leaderboard data according to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • App Store (In-App Purchases): Purchase transactions are processed by Apple. We do not access your payment information.

Local Data

Game settings (volume, sensitivity) and high scores are stored locally on your device. They are never transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Our games do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.
